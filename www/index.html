About Weimar

Weimar Tourist Information at Markt 10 is the first port of call for visitors to Weimar – and is also popular among local residents! The staff can help you with all your tourism enquiries – and if you’ve any requests, they’ll do their utmost to find a solution. As well as the tourist services provided by weimar GmbH, Weimar Tourist Information is also the place to go to see representatives of the Weimar Classic Foundation, the Buchenwald Memorial Society and Weimar District Council. We look forward to seeing you!
